Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2, 2025

Information We Collect

When you make a donation through Sevakosh, we collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Payment details (processed securely through our payment gateway)
  • Donation amount and purpose
  • IP address and browser information for security purposes

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process your donation and provide receipts
  • Communicate about your donation and our initiatives
  • Improve our services and website
  • Comply with legal requirements

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. All payment transactions are encrypted using SSL technology.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With payment processors to complete transactions
  • When required by law
  • With your explicit consent

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out of communications

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through our website or email.
