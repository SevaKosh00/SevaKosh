The Heart of Giving

Donations are a driving force for change, bridging the gap between privilege and need. Beyond financial aid, giving nurtures empathy, strengthens communities, and fosters a shared sense of responsibility. It is a reflection of humanity’s highest values—compassion, kindness, and the desire to uplift others.

Vision

SevaKosh is more than a platform—it is a movement redefining philanthropy with transparency, accountability, and impact. By channeling collective generosity into structured giving, it ensures that every contribution is utilized efficiently, creating meaningful change.

Why SevaKosh?

At SevaKosh, we believe that giving should be meaningful, transparent, and community-driven. Unlike traditional donation platforms, SevaKosh empowers society members to directly choose and support verified causes, ensuring every rupee makes a real difference. We operate with complete transparency—sharing bills, reports, and updates with every donor. Our model prioritizes accountability by involving society representatives in every stage of the donation process. With a strong focus on impact and trust, SevaKosh is not just a platform—it's a movement to make charitable giving more honest, efficient, and impactful for everyone involved.

Our Work

At SevaKosh, we focus on creating meaningful impact through small, thoughtful actions. Our initiatives center around the distribution of basic necessities, supporting child welfare programs, and assisting old age homes. With the help of society members and trusted local partners, we ensure that every donation reaches where it’s needed most. From providing daily essentials to creating safe spaces for the vulnerable, our work reflects a shared commitment to compassion and care.

Our Team

SevaKosh is driven by a passionate and committed team of individuals who believe in the power of collective giving. Our members come from diverse backgrounds—ranging from social work and community engagement to tech and management—but are united by a common goal: to make philanthropy more transparent, impactful, and community-oriented. We work closely with society representatives, volunteers, and donors to ensure that every initiative is thoughtfully planned and effectively executed. With a strong sense of purpose and collaboration, our team strives to turn compassion into action, one project at a time. Meet the people behind SevaKosh on the “Our Team” page.
